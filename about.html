ZARECON 2026

Advancing Zambia's Renewable Energy Future

The Zambia Annual Renewable Energy Conference (ZARECon) is the premier gathering for renewable energy professionals, policymakers, researchers, investors, and enthusiasts. Established to address Zambia's energy challenges and opportunities, ZARECon will become the definitive platform for knowledge exchange, networking, and collaboration in the renewable energy sector. ZARECon 2026 marks a significant milestone in our journey towards a sustainable energy future for Zambia. With the theme "Powering Sustainable Development: Innovation, Investment, and Implementation," this conference will focus on practical solutions and actionable strategies to accelerate Zambia's energy transition.

Our Mission

Promote renewable energy adoption in Zambia through collaboration and supportive policy development.

Vision

To be the leading driver of renewable energy transformation in Zambia and the region, ensuring reliable, affordable, and sustainable energy access for all.

Values

Guided by sustainability, innovation, integrity, collaboration, and inclusivity to advance clean, resilient energy in Zambia.

Our Impact Commitment

Accelerating Zambia’s clean energy transition through innovation, partnerships, and sustainable solutions that support economic growth and climate resilience.
