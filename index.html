ZARECON

Join us in shaping the future of renewable energy in Zambia

The Zambia Annual Renewable Energy Conference (ZARECon) is the premier gathering for renewable energy professionals, policymakers, researchers, and enthusiasts. Our mission is to foster collaboration, share knowledge, and accelerate the adoption of sustainable energy solutions across Zambia. This conference focuses on innovative technologies, policy frameworks, and investment opportunities in solar, wind, hydro, and biomass energy. We'll explore how renewable energy can drive economic growth, create jobs, and improve energy access for all Zambians. Join us for three days of insightful presentations, networking opportunities, and exhibitions showcasing the latest advancements in renewable energy technology.

Submit Your Research for ZARECon 2026

Call for Papers

Conference Theme

“POWERING ZAMBIA'S FUTURE: PIONEERING POLICY, INNOVATION, AND PARTNERSHIPS FOR ENERGY SECURITY – FROM POLICY TO ACTION.”

The Zambia Renewable Energy Association (ZARENA) invites researchers, practitioners, policy makers, and innovators to submit abstracts for presentation at the inaugural Zambia Annual Renewable Energy Conference (ZARECon) 2026.

Thematic Areas

  • Renewable Energy Technologies and Innovation
  • Energy–Water–Food Nexus
  • Climate Resilience and Inclusive Financing Models
  • Green Hydrogen Production and Utilisation
  • Energy Storage Systems (Battery, Thermal, Hydrogen, Pumped Hydro)
  • Smart Grids, IoT and Data-Driven Energy Systems
  • Policy, Regulation, and Climate Finance

Submission Guidelines

  • Format: Microsoft Word, A4, Times New Roman 11pt, single spacing
  • Margins: 35mm (left), 25mm (top, right, bottom)
  • Word Limit: Maximum 300 words (including figures, tables, references)
  • Required Sections:
  • Title
  • Author(s) name, institution, contact information
  • Abstract (max 300 words)
  • Five (5) keywords
  • Preferred presentation category

Submission Email: submissions@zarenayp.org

Important Dates

  • Call for Abstracts Opens: 21 October 2025
  • Abstract Submission Deadline: 31 December 2025
  • Notification of Acceptance: 15 January 2026
  • Final Abstract Submission: 15 February 2026
  • Conference Dates: Last Week of March 2026

Peer Review Process

All abstracts will be peer-reviewed by experts from:

  • University of Zambia (UNZA)
  • Copperbelt University
  • Strathclyde University (GRC Network)
  • ZARENA Technical Committee

Accepted authors will be invited to submit full papers for publication in the Conference Proceedings.
